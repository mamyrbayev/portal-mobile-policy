1. General

Portal Mobile is an official mobile wrapper for the corporate portal https://portal.petroleum.com.kz.

The iOS application itself does not collect or store any personal data on the device.

2. Data processed by the app itself

The app does not request login/password, does not use analytics, advertising, push notifications, in-app purchases, location, camera or any other permissions.

3. Data processed on the web portal

After logging into the website inside the app the following data may be processed by the portal:

  • employee login/ID and corporate email
  • profile data, documents and workflow data
  • cookies and session tokens
  • IP address and connection details

All these data belong to your organization and are processed solely on the portal servers.

4. Data sharing

Data is transmitted only to https://portal.petroleum.com.kz via HTTPS. No third-party sharing except as required by law.
